Common questions

Where does the news come from? Cards summarize public reporting by football journalists and news outlets. Each card names the credited journalist/outlet and links to the source.

Why didn't I get a notification? Check that notifications are enabled for ShimShim in your device settings. Medical-stage updates are intentionally silent.

A card looks wrong. News evolves and reporting can be corrected — cards reflect reporting at publication time. Tell us and we'll review it.
