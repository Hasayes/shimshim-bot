Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

What ShimShim is

ShimShim is a football transfer news reader. It shows summarized transfer news cards compiled from public reporting, with attribution and links to the original sources.

Data we collect

None. ShimShim has no accounts, no analytics, no ads and no tracking. The app downloads a public news feed and stores your display preferences only on your device.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, your device registers with Apple's and/or Google's push services so news alerts can be delivered. We store no personal information alongside this; notifications are broadcast to all subscribers and contain only news content. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

Third parties

The news feed is hosted on GitHub Pages. Push delivery uses Apple Push Notification service and/or Firebase Cloud Messaging. These services see standard technical delivery data (such as device push tokens) as part of operating; we do not receive or store it.
